PASTRIES

A selection of expertly handcrafted
pastries made with real butter and
premium ingredients baked fresh every

croissants, seasonal danish and ham &
cheese croissants.

LUNCH

Available daily.

Inspired by the Fraser Valley's produce
and meats, sandwiches on house made
bread rotate through the seasons.
Wholesome soups and salads are
periodically featured as part of a select
menu — check out our social media for
availability.

BEVERAGES

Our custom roasted beans are
thoughtfully crafted by Republica Coffee
Roasters in Fort Langley, so whether it's
an Americano, a cappuccino or a latte
paired with one of our house made
syrups, we've got you covered. Seasonal
flavours, teas and juices are also
available.
